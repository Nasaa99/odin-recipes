Steak

Description

Juicy, tender, and packed with bold flavor, this Ultimate Ribeye Steak is seared to perfection and finished with a rich garlic herb butter. The recipe keeps things simple with just a few ingredients—ribeye, olive oil, garlic, butter, rosemary, and thyme—yet delivers steakhouse-quality results right from your kitchen. Whether you're new to cooking steak or a seasoned home chef, this recipe breaks it down step by step for foolproof success.

The key to this mouthwatering ribeye lies in its preparation: a golden-brown crust from a hot skillet, aromatic herbs infused into melted butter, and a final spoon-baste that locks in flavor and moisture. Served hot with your favorite sides, this dish transforms an everyday dinner into something truly special—all in under an hour.

Ingredients

  • 1 pound ribeye steak
  • Salt and pepper (to taste)
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1/2 cup unsalted butter
  • 8 whole garlic cloves, peeled
  • Fresh rosemary sprigs
  • Fresh thyme sprigs

Steps

  1. Let the ribeye steak rest at room temperature for 30 minutes. Season both sides generously with salt and pepper.
  2. In a medium-sized skillet over medium-high heat, add olive oil. Once it starts to smoke, place the steak in the skillet.
  3. Sear the steak on all sides until a golden-brown crust forms.
  4. Add butter to the skillet and let it melt. Then add the garlic cloves, rosemary, and thyme sprigs.
  5. Reduce the heat to medium and continue cooking until the steak reaches your desired internal temperature.
  6. Spoon the melted butter and herbs over the steak repeatedly while it finishes cooking to enhance flavor and tenderness.
  7. Remove the steak from the skillet and let it rest for a few minutes before slicing and serving.
